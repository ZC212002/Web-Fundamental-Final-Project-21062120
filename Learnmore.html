Here's Our Raiders Designated For You!

Beginner gym workout for males

This workout is for men who want to gain strength and muscle mass. This is a beginner's full-body workout with an emphasis on the arms and core. By the end of this plan, you should have noticed changes in your body shape and all of your numbers (reps or weight lifted) on the exercises should have increased nearly every week. This guide will assist you in getting started.
  • Seated chest press (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Seated rows (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Wide grip lat pulldown (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Seated leg press (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Dumbbell seated shoulder press (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Dumbbell bicep curls (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Close grip tricep press ups (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Cable rotations/twists (10 reps x 4 sets)
  • Reverse crunches (10 reps x 4 sets)

Beginner gym workout for females

This female-only workout focuses on toning the entire body, with a focus on the legs and glutes (bottom) It's a myth that lifting weights will make women bulky. Weight lifting does, in fact, assist women in becoming stronger, leaner, and toned. This guide will assist you in getting started.
  • Seated leg press (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Seated shoulder press (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Close grip lat pulldown (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Bodyweight lunges (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Full/kneeling press ups (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Plank (30 secs x 3)
  • Leg raises (10 reps x 3 sets)

Beginner gym workout for fat loss

This workout will increase your heart rate and make you sweat. The goal here is to improve your cardiovascular fitness because a higher heart rate burns more calories. It also burns calories after you've finished the workout because your body is still working hard to get back to its normal state of function. So you're basically burning more calories while you're resting just to make your heart work a little harder during your workout. This guide will assist you in getting started.
  • Plate thrusters (15 reps x 3 sets)
  • Mountain climbers (20 reps x 3 sets)
  • Box jumps (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Walk outs (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Renegade rows (full plank/kneeling) (10 each side x 3 sets)
  • Press ups (full plank/kneeling) (15 reps x 3 sets)
  • Treadmill 10 min run/steep incline brisk walk (no hands)
  • Supermans (full plank/kneeling) (10 reps x 3 sets)
  • Crunches (10 reps x 3 sets)
